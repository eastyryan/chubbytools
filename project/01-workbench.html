The Pencil

Small details that make a big difference on site.

Every Chubby's pencil is designed around the actual moments carpenters, masons, and electricians live in — tight corners, awkward angles, and lost pencils.

01

Recycled, not replanted

Every pencil body is moulded from 100% recycled plastic. No virgin material, no felled timber. A first for the trade.

02

Integrated magnet

Snaps to steel components, scaffold, or the side of your tool belt. The pencil stays where you left it — even on a roof.

03

Graphite lead, 3× the life

We use real graphite, not pressed lead. It marks cleaner, sharpens easier, and lasts three times longer.

04

Built for tight corners

Originally designed for masons working near soffits — fits where your torpedo level can't. Then carpenters got hold of it.
